About

Step into Hideaway, an intimate and visually stunning nightlife destination tucked in the heart of Chicago’s Gold Coast. Designed for those who crave something beyond the ordinary, Hideaway blends art, sound, and sensation into one unforgettable experience. Behind its doors lies a world of ambient lighting, gold accents, and sculptural art installations—from illuminated walls of champagne bottles to intricate faces emerging from textured stone. Each detail is crafted to capture the essence of modern luxury and underground allure. Sip on signature craft cocktails and exclusive champagne selections, while the air pulses with the rhythm of commercial house and EDM. Whether you’re lounging beneath golden wings or dancing under hypnotic lights, every corner of Hideaway invites discovery. This isn’t just a club—it’s an experience. It’s your Hideaway.
