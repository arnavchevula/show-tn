# App Name Ideas

## Top Candidates

### Doortime
Already in the codebase as a scraped field (`doorsTime`). Refers to when doors open at a venue — specific to the live experience, no major brand owns it, implies "what's happening tonight."

### Opener
Same "pre-show ritual" angle as Soundcheck. Clean, specific to live music (the opening act). Nothing major using it currently. Check domain availability.

### Setlist
Clean, immediately understood by anyone who goes to shows. Implies you know what's playing before you go. **Caveat:** setlist.fm is well-established and may create SEO/brand confusion.

## Others Considered

| Name | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Soundcheck** | Real part of the live music process, not generic. Taken by a Nashville venue, and Apple used it in iTunes. SEO noise likely. |
| **The Bill** | As in "who's on the bill" — industry shorthand, clean. |
| **Handbill** | Old-school term for concert flyers, distinctive, nobody using it at scale. |
| **Marquee** | Already a NYC nightclub and used by a dozen apps. Avoid. |
| **Headliners** | Sounds like a news aggregator. Corny. |
| **Encore** | Overused by local music brands everywhere. |

## Domain Tips

If the `.com` is taken, these TLDs fit a music app and feel intentional rather than a fallback:
- `.fm`
- `.live`
- `.app`

e.g. `doortime.fm`, `opener.live`, `opener.fm`

Check availability at Namecheap or GoDaddy. Also check the App Store and Play Store if going mobile.
